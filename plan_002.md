## Simplified Public Forum

1. opening_constructive: speaker of proposition (this round is interactive only if the player is the proposition side)
2. opening_constructive: speaker of opposition
3. crossfire: speaker of opposition raise a question about the proposition opening_constructive
4. crossfire: speaker of proposition raise a question about the opposition opening_constructive
5. Rebuttal: speaker of proposition addresses opposition arguments
6. Rebuttal: speaker of opposition addresses proposition arguments
7. crossfire: speaker of proposition raise a question
8. crossfire: speaker of opposition raise a question
9. closing_constructive: speaker of proposition focuses on proposition key points
10. closing_constructive: speaker of opposition focuses on opposition key points
