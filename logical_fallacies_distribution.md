# Logical Fallacies Distribution

## Tutorials

Whenever a new fallacy is introduced to the player, a debate is dedicated to that fallacy.
Few other fallacy types will be used in the same debate, since the debate's goal is to teach about the newly introduced fallacy.

## Distribution

There are 3 types of fallacies, and every fallacy used in this game belongs to one of these 3 (check this file for details [logical_fallacies_distribution](./logical_fallacies_intro.md) ).

- Level 1: Emotional & Social
- Level 2: Faulty Logic & Connections
- Level 3: Structural & Conceptual

Complexity increases with Level number.
The game introduces fallacies from Level 1 alternating them with fallacies from Level 2.
Once 70% of Level 1 and Level 2 fallacies are taught, the game will introduce Level 3 fallacies.
